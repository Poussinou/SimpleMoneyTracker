A simple personal accounting app designed to make it easy to keep track of your income and expenses on your Android device.

<b>Features:</b><ul>
<li>Fast and easy entry and editing of income and expenses.</li>
<li>Summary balance sheet and income/expense statement shows you where you stand on selected period.</li>
<li>Easy reconciliation with your bank statements.</li>
<li>Import/Export .csv files.</li></ul>
